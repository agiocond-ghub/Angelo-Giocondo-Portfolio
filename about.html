About Me

My name is Angelo Giocondo and I am a photographer and Graphic Information Technology student at Arizona State University. I enjoy combining artistic creativity and technology through film photography and editing.

My interest in photography first spiked a few years ago when I started bringing my camera with me to concerts to take photos and videos casually. As time went on, my interest grew and I began expanding into the world of film photography. I have since consistently learned new techniques and moved into the direction of nature and lifestyle photography.
