Photography Gear

Here are the 2 main cameras I use for digital and film photography.

Sony A6000

My primary digital camera for concert, everyday carrying, and videography. Despite its age, the A6000 has been an absolute workhorse for me due to its interchangeable lense and attachment capabilities.

Canon AE-1 Program

My favorite 35mm film camera. This camera has been such a joy to learn film photography on. Whether it be the auto mode this model offers, or manually adjusting settings to compensate for exposure, this camera has changed the way I look at photography as a whole and I don't plan on upgrading anytime soon.
